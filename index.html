Welcome to the NBA

The National Basketball Association (NBA) is a men's professional basketball league in North America, consisting of 30 teams (29 in the United States and 1 in Canada). Founded in 1946, the NBA has grown into a global sports and entertainment brand with a massive following of fans around the world. The league features some of the most talented basketball players in the world, competing in a regular season that runs from October to April, followed by playoffs to determine the NBA champion. The NBA is known for its fast-paced, high-scoring games, and its players are celebrated for their athleticism, skill, and star power. Off the court, the NBA is also a leader in social and cultural issues, with players and teams using their platforms to advocate for social justice, equality, and other important causes.
